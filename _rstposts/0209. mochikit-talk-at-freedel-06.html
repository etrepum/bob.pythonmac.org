Specifically it covers JavaScript web application development with `MochiKit`_ and `Dojo`_, and it's split into four sets of slides. I've just had a chance to give them a look and they're quite good. The slides are all done with `S5`_, which is what I used for the `MochiKit Intro`_ talk I gave a few months ago. `Part 1 - JavaScript`_: A general overview of JavaScript and AJAX `Part 2 - MochiKit`_: An overview of what `MochiKit`_ is and why you'd want to use it. Lots of pointers to cool features such as `DOM`_ and `Signal`_. `Part 3 - Dojo`_: Covers a bunch of the great functionality that `Dojo`_ has that isn't available anywhere else such as widgets, layout, animation, and offline storage. `Part 4 - Case Studies`_: A few very short case studies of some JavaScript web applications. .. _`DOM`: http://mochikit.com/doc/html/MochiKit/DOM.html .. _`Signal`: http://mochikit.com/doc/html/MochiKit/Signal.html .. _`S5`: http://meyerweb.com/eric/tools/s5/ .. _`MochiKit Intro`: http://bob.pythonmac.org/archives/2006/05/11/mochikit-intro-ajax-experience-slides/ .. _`Part 1 - JavaScript`: http://www.kix.in/webapps/part1.html .. _`Part 2 - MochiKit`: http://www.kix.in/webapps/part2.html .. _`Part 3 - Dojo`: http://www.kix.in/webapps/part3.html .. _`Part 4 - Case Studies`: http://www.kix.in/webapps/part4.html .. _`Dojo`: http://www.dojotoolkit.org/ .. _`MochiKit`: http://mochikit.com/ .. _`Anant Narayanan`: http://www.kix.in/ .. _`Web Application Development`: http://www.kix.in/webapps/ .. _`Freedel 06`: http://www.freedel.in/
